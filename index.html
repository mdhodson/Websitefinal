Hello!

My name is McKayla Hodson. I was born and raised in Folsom, California (yes, where the famous song, "Folsom Prison Blues" by Johnny Cash was coined.)

We've got a beautiful lake there that my family and I would go paddleboarding on every summer! We would spend hours out on the lake in the hot California sun.

I have 14 people in my family: 3 brothers, 1 sister, 2 sister-in-laws, 1 brother-in law, 3 nieces, 1 nephew, and 2 parents! (Can't forget my dog too:))

I love to play the guitar, sing, run, be outside, and hangout with my friends! I recently returned home from my mission in Montana and Wyoming. It was a wonderful expereince to be able to serve and teach the people there.

I am so excited to be back at BYU and continue in my education! As you look around my website you'll see some of the things I have accomplished and expereinced during my life. Hope you enjoy!
